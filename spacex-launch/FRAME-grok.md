# Grok HyperFrames — frame.md

Authoring unit: 1920 x 1080.

This is the frame guide for the Grok adaptation of the Claude launch cut. `design.md` is the canonical palette; this file captures how those atoms are used at video scale.

## Brand Atoms

- Canvas: `#030404`
- Sidebar/chrome: `#070807`
- Panel: `#171717`
- Elevated panel: `#202020`
- Pressed/selected panel: `#303030`
- Ink: `#F4F4F0`
- Muted: `#A7A7A2`
- Quiet: `#74746F`
- Border: `#2B2B2B`
- Soft border: `#252525`
- Accent: `#F4F4F0`
- Accent text: `#F4F4F0`
- On accent: `#030404`
- HyperFrames cyan: `#06E3FA`
- HyperFrames green: `#4FDB5E`
- Recording edge: `#184C3C`

## Typography

- UI/display: `"Hanken Grotesk", system-ui, sans-serif`
- Long answer/editorial: `"Newsreader", Georgia, serif`
- Tool labels: `"Spline Sans Mono", monospace`

Use large video-native sizes: 60px+ for major text, 28px+ for readable body, 18px+ for labels. Keep the UI compact but not web-sized.

## Composition Rules

- The first frame must signal Grok immediately without faking a full Grok home-screen background: black canvas, compact Grok lockup, dark composer/menu surfaces, and connector context.
- Menus are dark panels, not light popovers.
- Keep Grok UI accents monochrome. Use cyan/green only inside the HyperFrames connector mark or progress details.
- Preserve the original Claude cut's timing and transition choreography unless the visual state demands a small adjustment.
- Keep the final composer in scene 1 visually aligned with scene 2 for the hard cut.

## Recurring Components

- Composer: `#171717` fill, `#252525` border, `#A7A7A2` placeholder, rounded 2.4cqw.
- Connector menu: `#202020` panel, `#303030` hover/selected row, `#2B2B2B` divider.
- Toggle on: `#F4F4F0` track with `#030404` knob.
- Tool card: dark panel with mono pills and the HyperFrames by HeyGen mark.
- Outro: white Grok lockup with a restrained cool connector trace, held on black canvas.

## Avoid

- Cream or paper tones from the Claude source.
- Claude-specific clay language or logo data.
- Orange/SuperGrok sales-offer UI.
- Generic blue/purple AI styling.
- Empty centered text without app chrome or connector proof.
