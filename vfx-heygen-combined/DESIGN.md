# HeyGen iPhone Canvas Test

## Style Prompt

A compact product-device test: a single black iPhone floats in a dark studio while the screen shows a crisp mobile version of HeyGen's homepage. The page texture should feel like a live site capture: white panels, black editorial typography, lime-green calls to action, compact stats, and product feature cards. The motion is polished but restrained, designed to prove the device and screen pipeline rather than tell a full ad story.

## Colors

- `#050508` - studio background
- `#f7f4ee` - HeyGen-style page canvas
- `#111111` - primary text
- `#d7ff3f` - CTA lime
- `#4d78ff` - product blue accent
- `#ff7a59` - warm feature accent

## Typography

- `Inter`, `system-ui`, sans-serif
- Large condensed-ish hero type through heavy weight and tight line height

## What Not To Do

- Do not add the liquid glass lens or any translucent object over the phone screen.
- Do not introduce a laptop or secondary device.
- Do not use a generic app UI; the screen should read as HeyGen's public homepage.
- Do not rely on external website embedding for the phone texture.
